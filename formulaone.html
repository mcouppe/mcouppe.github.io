TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION

camera : Nikon D750
lens : Nikon 18-35mm, Nikon 50mm and Nikon 85mm
date : january 2017
fashion designer : Solenn Roux
MUA : Alice Cottet

This series was commissioned to illustrate a designer's collection. This collection was inspired by the Formula 1 driver Niki Lauda, his world where competition never stopped even in his tumultuous personal life, especially when he competed against James Hunt in 1976. A rising Formula One star, a good amount of love scandals, a tumultuous backstage scene hidden behind the spotlights of the front of the stage are all the ingredients explored in this collection. It's also a game about gender roles : the man becomes fragile and defeated while the woman, strong in her leather jacket, chooses the winning side.

This was my first group shoot, with several models to direct. It involved thinking about how light can make a conversation in the silence and stillness of photography. How to show what unites and disunites two characters? A play of glances and lights is then instated.

The atmosphere is dark, heavy, heavy, almost vitiated while the models are shining stars with their share of light and shadow captured by the photograph.
